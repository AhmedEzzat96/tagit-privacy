🧾 Tag!t – Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Thank you for using Tag!t.
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (“the App”).

🧍‍♂️ 1. Information We Collect

Tag!t does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.
All data (such as tags, notes, or items) is saved locally on your device only and is never uploaded to any external server.

We do not collect:

  • Names, emails, or contact information
  • Location or GPS data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Photos, files, or any personal content

🔒 2. How Your Data Is Used

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, Tag!t does not use or share any user information. Everything you create remains private to your device.

🌍 3. Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party SDKs or services that collect user data (such as analytics, ads, or crash reporting).

⚙️ 4. Data Storage & Deletion

All information is stored only on your local device.
If you delete the app, all locally stored data is automatically deleted and cannot be recovered.

👶 5. Children’s Privacy

Tag!t is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.
